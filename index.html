Global Privacy Policy for All Applications

DalJord Creations ("we," "us," or "our") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications and games developed and published by DalJord Creations on the Google Play Store and other platforms.

Our applications are built as Ad-Supported or Freemium services. This SERVICE is provided by DalJord Creations at no cost and is intended for use as is. If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way.

However, our applications use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to provide services like analytics, advertising, and crash reporting.

2. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party partners to improve our services and display advertising. These partners may collect data such as your Device ID, Advertising ID, IP address, and in-game usage data. Please review the privacy policies of the services we use:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (Analytics)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)

3. Detailed Data Practices

Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID (AAID) to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

Analytics & Game Data (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how our apps are used (e.g., how many users passed a level, average session length). This data is anonymized and helps us fix bugs and improve gameplay. We also use Firebase Remote Config to update game parameters without an update and Firebase Cloud Messaging to send notifications.

Log Data

In a case of an error in the app, we collect data and information (through third-party products) on your phone called Log Data. This may include your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our Service, the time and date of your use of the Service, and other statistics.

4. Cookies

Our Service does not use "cookies" explicitly. However, third-party code and libraries used in our apps (like AdMob) may use "cookies" to collect information and improve their services. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies within your device or browser settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
