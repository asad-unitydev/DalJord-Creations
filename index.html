DalJord Creations ("we," "us," or "our") is dedicated to protecting the privacy of our users ("you" or "your"). This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, games, and digital services developed and published by DalJord Creations.

Our applications are built as Ad-Supported or Freemium services. This SERVICE is provided by DalJord Creations at no cost and is intended for use as is. If you choose to use our Service, then you agree to the collection and use of information in relation to this policy.

1. Information Collection

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information. The information that we request will be retained on your device and is not collected by us in any way.

However, our applications integrate trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you to provide core functionalities like analytics, advertising, and crash reporting.

2. Third-Party Partners

We partner with industry-leading providers to ensure our games run smoothly and remain free. Please review the privacy policies of the services we utilize:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob (Advertising Network)
  • Google Analytics for Firebase (User Insights)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Stability Monitoring)

3. Data Usage Breakdown

Advertising (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID (AAID) to serve personalized advertisements based on your interests. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).

Game Analytics (Firebase)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand user behavior (e.g., level progression, session duration). This data is anonymized and helps us balance game difficulty and improve features. We also use Firebase Remote Config to deliver content updates and Firebase Cloud Messaging for game notifications.

Log Data

In the event of an app error, we collect Log Data (via third-party products). This may include your device Internet Protocol (“IP”) address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, and timestamp.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our Service does not use "cookies" explicitly. However, third-party libraries (like AdMob) may use cookies or similar technologies to collect information. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies within your device settings.

5. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

6. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
